HELLDIVERS 2

Released in 2024, Helldivers 2 took the gaming world by storm, quickly becoming PlayStation's fastest selling title of all time. This co-op third person shooter perfectly blends together unique gameplay elements by featuring "Stratagems", powerful equipment that are activated by pressing a series of directional keys - making the fight for democracy and freedom a chaotically fun scramble across your keyboard.
