SUPER SMASH BROS ULTIMATE

The latest entry to the storied Super Smash Bros franchise, this chaotic and wacky platform fighter is the largest yet - featuring a staggering 89 playable fighters. With this many characters to choose from (including Joker from Persona 5!) and up to 8 players allowed in a single game, Smash Ultimate is the perfect party game for casuals and pros alike.
