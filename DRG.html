DEEP ROCK GALACTIC

Danger. Darkness. Dwarves. This is the motto of Deep Rock Galactic, a 2020 co-op shooter where you play as a dwarf miner employed by Deep Rock Galactic, a mining corporation that sends its employees deep down into the caves of Hoxxes IV to extract resources. With procedurally generated caves and a wealth of potential enemies and mission objectives, each mining expedition in Deep Rock is bound to be a new and exciting adventure.
