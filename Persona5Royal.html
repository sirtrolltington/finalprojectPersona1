PERSONA 5 ROYAL

Persona 5 Royal is the game that inspired this entire project! Released in 2020, Persona 5 Royal strikes the perfect balance between being a nuanced social-sim and a traditional JRPG with an incredibly engaging combat system. What I especially love about Persona 5 Royal is its overall design - everything, from its jazz and soul soundtrack to its bold and flash menus absolutely ooze style and make for an amazing gaming experience.
